⚙️ How it works

Up and running in three steps

01

Configure your receptionist

Name your AI, define your organisation, set its knowledge base — visiting hours, services, contacts, FAQs — and choose its personality. Takes minutes, no technical knowledge needed.

02

Embed on your website

Add a single script tag to your website. The holographic receptionist appears as a floating widget, ready to engage every visitor. Works on any website, any platform.

03

It handles the rest

Your receptionist answers questions 24/7, speaks aloud using voice synthesis, accepts voice input, and escalates complex queries to your team via WhatsApp or email. Fully automated.
